Mardi Gras

Mardi Gras, also known as Fat Tuesday, is a vibrant celebration that takes place before Lent, particularly famous in New Orleans, Louisiana. The festival features lively parades, colorful floats, elaborate costumes, and music, creating a festive atmosphere. Participants often indulge in rich foods, and traditional treats like King Cake are enjoyed. Mardi Gras is marked by a spirit of revelry and community, drawing visitors from around the world to join in the festivities, which often begin weeks before the main event.
