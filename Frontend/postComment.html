Look at them cheese 🤤

The cheese festival was an amazing experience! The air was filled with mouthwatering aromas as I sampled a variety of cheeses from local artisans. Chatting with cheesemakers was a highlight, and the tasting sessions introduced me to delicious pairings with wine. The lively atmosphere, complete with music and families enjoying the day, made it all the more fun. I even took a cheese-making workshop! I can’t wait for next year’s festival!
